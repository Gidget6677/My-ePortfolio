Project Idea

Overview

The project I have chosen is a group travel app that will make it easier to plan a trip among friends or family which is something that can be made tricky when trying to figure out people’s individual budget limits, itinerary preferences, and everything else needed for a great a trip. This app is going to be designed to help keep everyone on the same page by organizing itineraries, expenses, and photos all on one app for everyone to see and provide input decreasing the chances of people cancelling out of frustration and guaranteeing an amazing trip for everyone involved.

Motivation

What inspired me to make this app was that recently I was asked to go on a road trip with a group of friends which I was excited to go to till I remember the numerous amounts of times where I have planned vacations with friends only for them to fall apart a week down the line for several different reasons. To try and prevent this I researched for apps that would assist us with planning the trip, but I couldn’t find any that would fit me, and my friends need. While doing research for this I found that 85% of travellers use mobile devices to book travel activities. (Martech Zone, 2018) and more than not it’s several different apps all scattered around such as trivago and booking.com so I figured why not do all the things they can do but on one app. In addition, travel agent central found that 40% of individuals want to travel with friends but decide not to because of the difficulties involved in the task.

Discription

The app itself will have several different features and services ranging from allowing the users to suggest possible dates within the app and then all your mates can take a vote. The idea will be to use a poll feature at the end which would be a good way to decide fairly without anyone kicking off that they were left out.

The app will make sharing expenses simple. It will have an option where you can just fill in the costs (food, shopping, accommodation etc) and the app does the maths for you. Invite your group members who will be borrowing money into a group chat so they can log their own expenses and check out what others owe. Anything owed to you is highlighted in green with a (+) sign, and the money that you owe is highlighted in red with a (-). No more handing each other the same fiver back and forth until the end of time.

On top of that this app will also allow you to create polls for destinations and budget (and anything else you might need to vote on) rather than just dates. You can also easily gather everyone’s travel information – things like full names, dates of birth and allergies in one place. You can also get information like if a person has a passport and whether they can travel out of state.

Flight comparison is another feature that will be included on the app which will help users find cheap flights from their sofa, allowing everyone to communicate what they want, and they can and can’t afford and making sure that they are all on the same flight. Also, the app will allow the users to search destinations by budget and travel timeframe. This is a great way to find destinations you might never have thought of travelling to. You can set up reliable flight alerts and can also compare hotels and car hire options all at the same time as well. Another unique feature of the app will be a local culture snap section which will include short to in-depth travel guides for each destination that will focus on location-centred stories and activities

in different locations worldwide. In addition, I hope that the articles themselves will mostly be written by local experts who can really give the user a flavour for the place their going. I also plan to have an itinerary segment where the user can create their own individual itinerary for each location and locate restaurants, bars, pubs, and markets in a specific location.

Lastly the main feature that comes to mind will be an Activities section where you can find, compare, and book sightseeing tours, attractions, excursions, things to do and fun activities. It’s simple to use and clearly shows the highlights of each tour, and what exactly is included. Once you’ve selected your tour or activity, you can also set your budget for the activities and whether you want ones you can also book and pay in advance or pay when you get there.
